Privacy Policy — Digital OSP

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Digital OSP ("the App") is the official mobile application of the Ophthalmological Society of Pakistan ("OSP", "we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Who this app is for

Digital OSP is intended for members of the Ophthalmological Society of Pakistan and other ophthalmology professionals. An account is required to access content.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided when you sign in with Google or with an email and password.
  • Authentication identifiers — a unique user ID issued by Firebase Authentication.
  • Content you create — posts, comments, images and other material you submit to the community feed or academic sections.
  • Device & usage data — basic app-interaction data and crash/diagnostic reports used to keep the App stable.
  • Push notification token — to deliver notifications you have enabled.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not collect payment information.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate you.
  • To display the news feed, conferences, academic resources and community content.
  • To send you push notifications about OSP news and events.
  • To moderate community content and keep the platform safe.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.

4. Service providers

We use the following third-party services, which process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Realtime Database, Cloud Messaging, Crashlytics) — see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare (R2 object storage and Workers) — for storing and serving images, PDFs and other media.

These providers process data under their own privacy and security terms.

5. Data sharing

Content you post to the community or academic sections is visible to other authenticated OSP members within the App. We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising.

6. Data security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Access to stored data is restricted by Firebase Security Rules and authentication.

7. Data retention and deletion

We retain your account data while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us at the address below. Some content may be retained where required for moderation or legal reasons.

8. Children

The App is intended for medical professionals and is not directed to children under 18.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
