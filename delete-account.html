Ophthalmological Society of Pakistan · Last updated: 24 June 2026

Digital OSP is the official mobile application of the Ophthalmological Society of Pakistan (OSP). This page explains how to request deletion of your account and the personal data associated with it.

How to request account deletion

To request that your Digital OSP account and its associated data be deleted, send an email to the address below:

Email: asimsafeer75@gmail.com
  1. Email asimsafeer75@gmail.com from the email address registered to your account.
  2. Use the subject line "Digital OSP account deletion request".
  3. Include the name and email address associated with your account so we can verify your identity.
  4. We will verify the request and delete your account within 30 days, and confirm by email once complete.

What data is deleted

When your deletion request is processed, the following data is permanently deleted:

DataOutcome
Account profile (name, email, user ID)Deleted
Authentication record (Firebase Authentication)Deleted
Posts, images and other content you created in the community feedDeleted
Push notification tokenDeleted

What data may be retained

DataRetention
Records required for moderation, safety, or to comply with legal obligationsRetained only as long as required, then deleted
Anonymized or aggregated diagnostic data that can no longer identify youMay be retained
